About Me

I studied applied mathematics during my undergraduate years and graduated in May 2024. After graduation, I explored different paths and began self-directed learning through online courses while researching job opportunities.

My path to graduation and beyond hasn’t been easy, shaped by life events that have strengthened my resolve as a non-traditional student. A few months after graduating, I discovered a passion for data science and machine learning, and I’m now pursuing a career in these fields with curiosity and a growth mindset.

I value humility, kindness, and perceptiveness—traits I’ve learned from my grandparents and my dear husband. Whenever possible, I enjoy spending time with my loved one, connecting with nature, and finding balance through yoga, dance, and quiet, peaceful spaces. I also occasionally play chess and draw to unwind.
